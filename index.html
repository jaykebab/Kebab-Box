THE KEBAB BOX PROMISE

Quality Without Compromise

We believe high-speed dining should never sacrifice quality and safety. We stand by these 6 core parameters for every box served.

🍗

Fresh Chicken

We source premium quality, tender poultry daily. Our chicken is never frozen, ensuring succulent textures and natural juices in every bite.

🌻

No Palm Oil

Your health is our utmost priority. We fry and baste exclusively with premium sunflower oil, eliminating cheap palm oil substitutes entirely.

🧪

No Artificial Colours

That stunning reddish-orange charred hue? It comes entirely from real Kashmiri chillies and natural spice rubs. No chemical dyes permitted.

🧂

No Ajinomoto

We say no to MSG and artificial taste enhancers. We rely solely on natural, traditional marination, herbs, and spices to deliver deep flavor profiles.

🍳

Oil Changed Daily

No rancid, recycled oil. Our sunflower cooking oil is discarded and replaced fresh every single morning to guarantee lightness and clean digestion.

🧼

Clean & Hygienic

From UV-sanitized kitchen prep surfaces to touchless, sealed boxes, safety is our standard. Our kitchens observe rigorous sanitization procedures.

Clean • Fresh • High-Quality

That is the Kebab Box Promise. Freshness without shortcuts, flavor without chemicals.
